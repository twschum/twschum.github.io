Official Texture Pack

Endoc HD 64x64 Pack:

Having a unified texturepack means that everyone will see the same thing when they are playing, which can often make certain building/architectural choices make more sense. This is the only place to get HD textures for the EndocZ mod, and we have also built textures for the Bibliocraft mod to go with the pack. Additionally, there are higher-quality textures of the dragons in DragonMounts, and now also some key sounds and textures for the CustomNPSc mod.
Furthermore, this is the only place to get the HD textures for our homebrew EndocZ mod.

This pack itself is a continuation on Ovo's Rustic pack. Since then, we have maintained and added bits and pieces from other mods, as well as created many textures ourselves -see the readme.txt in the pack itself for details on credits, and the ChangeLog.txt for details about the new version.
